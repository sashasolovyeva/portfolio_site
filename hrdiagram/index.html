I thought that the Hertzprung-Russell diagram was pretty fascinating! You know, a quick handy chart that denotes a life cycle of every star from Sirius to the Alpha Centauri.

Wondering what will happen to our own Sun when the fusion stops and into explodes into a Red Giant? Will it ever become a Black Hole? Why or why not? This animated interactive experience will answer these questions and, hopefully, make you as obsessed with stellar life cycles as I am.
